Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 cup reduced fat sour cream
  • 1 ounce packet taco seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon mild chunky salsa
  • 2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • shredded lettuce
    • <1i>1 tomato, chopped

Steps

  1. Combine ranch dressing, sour cream, etc.
  2. Toss chicken with remaining taco seasoning.
  3. Warm tortillas in skillet for about a minute.
